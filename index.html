1. Information Collection

Hum aapka account banate waqt sirf zaroori maloomat lete hain:

  • ➢ Account Details: Aapka naam, email aur login info.
  • ➢ Gaming ID: Game verification ke liye (In-Game Name/ID).

2. Data Ka Istemal

Collected data ka istemal sirf in maqasid ke liye hota hai:

  • ➢ Tournament results aur points verify karne ke liye.
  • ➢ Winners ko rewards aur update notifications bhejne ke liye.
  • ➢ Platform par fair play aur safety barkarar rakhne ke liye.

3. Fair Play & Security

WS Battle hacking, modding ya unfair gameplay ke khilaf hai. Agar koi user rules todta hai, toh hamare paas ye haq mahfooz hai ke hum unka account permanent block kar dein.
